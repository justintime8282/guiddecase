# Guidde Sales Operations Manager Case Study Presentation Outline

## Format
Recommended format: a short guidde-recorded walkthrough, backed by a lightweight slide deck and HubSpot screenshots.

## Slide 1. Title
- Guidde Sales Operations Manager Case Study
- Justin [Last Name]
- Sales Ops design for a PLG-hybrid revenue motion

## Slide 2. Executive Summary
- Built around a PLG-hybrid GTM model
- PQL is the center of qualification logic
- Designed a 6-stage HubSpot pipeline
- Proposed 2 workflows: lifecycle + deal automation
- Included KPI dashboard for performance, bottlenecks, and revenue opportunities

## Slide 3. Why this model fits Guidde
- Guidde is best served by a PLG-hybrid motion
- Product behavior should drive qualification
- Lifecycle stage and deal stage should be managed separately
- Revenue ownership spans BDR, AE, and CS

## Slide 4. Lifecycle Model
- Subscriber → Lead → MQL → PQL → SQL → Opportunity → Customer
- PQL defined by product activation
- Example signals:
  - created a guidde
  - published a guidde
  - invited a teammate

## Slide 5. Pipeline Design
- SQL / New Opportunity
- Discovery & Demo
- Evaluation / Trial (POC)
- Proposal & Negotiation
- Verbal Commit / Contract Sent
- Closed Won / Closed Lost

## Slide 6. Workflow 1, Lead Lifecycle
- Trigger from signup, high-intent forms, and activation signals
- Promote contacts through lifecycle stages automatically
- Assign owner and notify rep at PQL
- Promote to SQL on demo booked, BDR qualification, or score threshold
- Use forward-only logic and lifecycle change stamping

## Slide 7. Workflow 2, Deal Automation
- Trigger on SQL, BDR qualification, or demo booked
- Dedupe first
- Create new deal if no open one exists
- Update existing deal if one already exists
- Create tasks, stamp fields, and maintain owner continuity

## Slide 8. Revenue Process Ownership
- BDR qualifies and routes
- AE owns discovery through close
- CS supports trial/onboarding and takes over on Closed Won
- Structured handoffs improve forecast trust and execution quality

## Slide 9. Deal Creation Process
- Human judgment determines qualification
- System creates and standardizes the deal
- Required fields at creation:
  - Deal Source
  - Primary Contact
  - ICP Fit
  - Use Case
  - Estimated Amount / ACV
  - Buying Group
  - Expected Timeline
  - Product Interest
- Accuracy controls: dedupe, incomplete-deal tasks, stale-deal alerts

## Slide 10. Risks, Bottlenecks, Opportunities
- Risks:
  - low-quality PQL over-routing
  - missed high-intent users
  - weak stage discipline
- Bottleneck:
  - Evaluation / Trial (POC) can stall without structured success criteria
- Opportunity:
  - outbound enrichment + ICP scoring as an appendix feeding the same SQL gate

## Slide 11. Dashboard and KPIs
- Pipeline & Forecasting:
  - pipeline by stage
  - weighted pipeline
  - forecast accuracy
  - average days in stage
- Sales Performance:
  - closed won by rep
  - win rate by rep
  - SQL to Closed Won conversion
  - average deal size
- Productivity & Efficiency:
  - time from PQL to first sales action
  - demo booked rate
  - trial to proposal conversion
  - follow-up SLA attainment
  - CS handoff completion rate
